Testing Protocal

To ensure every one of our CBD products meets our strict standards and ensure quality and purity, we have a 3rd party lab tests undertaken at every step of our process. All of our products have batch numbered COA’s (Certificate of Analysis).

All of our testings are done through ProVerde Laboratories as they are regarded as one of the best, accurate and reliable labs in the industry. ProVerde Laboratories is accredited under ISO/IEC 17025:2005.

The raw cannabis/hemp plant material is tested before processing to verify it’s not only clean and free of pesticides but meets our required standards. We also want to ensure we meet the legal requirement of <0.3% THC at dry weight before processing. After processing, the raw CBD oils are then sent away for another 3rd party lab test for validation of purity, cleanliness, potency and overall quality.

Once our CBD products come off the production line, we then send samples of each batch away for final batch tracked 3rd party testing to clarify the final product you receive meets our strict standard and is of the highest and purest quality.

We work very hard to create high quality, safe, clean and consistent CBD products for our family and our customers. We never cut corners; we never try to save a buck.
